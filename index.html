WELCOME TO RUD

Royal University of Dhaka (RUD) is a private institution of higher education offering four years undergraduate degrees in Business Administrator(BBA), Hotel Management & Tourism (HTM), English, Computer Science & information Technology (CSIT), Computer Science and Engineering(CSE), and Education(B.Ed). Our Post graduate programs are Master's in Business Administrator (MBA), Computer Science and Engineering (MSCSE), Library Management & Information Science (MSLMIS) and Education (M.Ed). YOU, the students, are our main concern at RUD. The aim of our academic and administrative staff is to ensure that each student achieves his/her academic Potential. The secure and nurturing environment of RUD is ideal for developing both personal and practical skills. Students who see their degree studies as a steeping stone to a successful professional career will find that RUD meets their needs. Whether you join us in your first year as fresh or transfer student or even as a postgraduate, you can be sure of receiving a very warm welcome it RUD.
